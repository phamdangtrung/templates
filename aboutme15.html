my story

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm a digital artist and web enthusiast who fell in love with the aesthetics of the late 90s and early 2000s.

Back when the web was young and full of possibilities, I discovered the magic of hand-drawn designs mixed with futuristic Y2K elements. The combination of sketchbook warmth and chrome-plated dreams captivated me.

This blog is my tribute to that era — a place where imperfect lines meet glossy interfaces, where paper textures blend with metallic gradients, and where every pixel tells a story of digital nostalgia.
