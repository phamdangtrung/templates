Welcome

Welcome to my digital space! This is where I share my thoughts, projects, and creative endeavors. The design blends the optimistic Frutiger Aero aesthetic with Y2K futurism.

The future looked bright in the early 2000s — glossy interfaces, nature-inspired colors, and the promise of a connected world. This site is a tribute to that era of digital optimism.
